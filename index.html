Event Schedule

Chronological course of the signature engagement evening.

5:00 PM Lobby Salon

Doors Open & Welcome Reception

Arrival of credentialed guests, custom cocktail service, and ambient string accompaniment.

6:00 PM Grand Library

Author Reading & Opening Remarks

First-edition reading segments presented by our featured authors inside the Grand Hall.

7:00 PM Lecture Hall

Panel Discussion: Future of the Written Word

In-depth roundtable discussion debating contemporary publishing, digital transformation, and cultural preservation.

8:00 PM Auditorium

Moderated Q&A Session

Audience participation session. Ask questions directly to the featured novelists and poets.

8:45 PM Rotunda Suite

Book Signing & Networking Gala

Secure author autographs on special editions, coupled with wine selections and closing relations.

Venue & Logistics

Essential details for planning your arrival at our physical ballroom.

The Literary Nexus

Portland Grand Ballroom, 421 SW Morrison St, Portland, OR 97204

Valet & Parking

Complimentary valet service is offered directly at the main entrance. Secured self-parking garages are positioned within a 2-block radius.

Transit Routes

Convenient access is available via the MAX Light Rail system, stopping directly at the Morrison/3rd Ave Station, positioned 1 block east.

Dress Protocol

Creative cocktail or smart business attire is highly encouraged for this cultural gathering.
